Our Story

We Write for Curious Minds

ClayBlog was founded in 2023 with a simple belief: the internet deserves more thoughtful, evidence-based writing. We cover technology, lifestyle, productivity, and design — not with clickbait headlines, but with depth and honesty.

Every article goes through rigorous fact-checking and editorial review. We don't publish filler. If we write about a tool or a practice, we've tested it ourselves and have something real to say.

We're a small, independent team of writers and researchers committed to quality over quantity. No investors. No content farms. Just writers who care about getting it right.
